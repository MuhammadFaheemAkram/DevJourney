# Code Of Conduct

DevJourney is a learning-oriented project. Contributors are expected to make the space useful, respectful, and welcoming for people with different levels of Android experience.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still asking clear questions.
- Keep feedback focused on code, documentation, design, or behavior.
- Welcome beginners and explain tradeoffs without condescension.
- Respect boundaries around time, availability, and maintainership.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive comments about identity, background, or skill level.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Any behavior that makes collaboration unsafe or hostile.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may lead to a permanent ban from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately through the security/contact process described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Maintainers will review reports in good faith and avoid sharing sensitive details beyond what is needed to respond.
